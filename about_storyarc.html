    My Story

    Hi, I’m Dave, the face behind StoryArc Creative. I’m a Washington, DC-based creative with a multidisciplinary background in product, visual storytelling and creative strategy. My passion to create is fueled by the opportunity to help tell powerful stories that in turn, help make a positive change in the world. Over the years I’ve found myself so in love with the creative process that it’s been impossible to define one area of “specialty” Throughout my career, I’ve immersed myself in different techniques and practices for visual storytelling and creative design, while honoring the best practices in the respective field. That’s why on this site you’ll find everything from product UX/UI, to animation, character design, visual branding, and more! Interested in working on a project together? Let's Chat
